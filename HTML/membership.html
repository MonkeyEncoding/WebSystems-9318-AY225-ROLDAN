Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Who can join JPCS?

+

JPCS membership is open to all students enrolled in computer science, information technology, or related programs. We welcome students of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced.

What's the difference between membership tiers?

+

Student Members get basic access to events and community. Active Members receive priority registration, exclusive workshops, and mentorship. Premium Members get all benefits plus 1-on-1 mentorship, industry projects, and career counseling.

How do I pay for membership?

+

After your application is approved, we'll send payment instructions via email. We accept bank transfers, GCash, and cash payments at our office during office hours.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade your membership at any time. Contact us at jpcs.official@university.edu.ph to process your upgrade.

What events can I attend as a member?

+

All members can attend our workshops, seminars, hackathons, and networking events. Active and Premium members get priority registration for limited-capacity events and exclusive workshops.
