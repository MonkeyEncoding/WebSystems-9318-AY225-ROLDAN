About JPCS

Leading the future of technology education in the Philippines

JPCS TEAM 2026

Empowering Future Tech Leaders

The Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS) is the premier student organization dedicated to fostering excellence in computer science and information technology. Since our founding, we have been at the forefront of technological innovation, providing students with opportunities to learn, grow, and excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

We believe in creating a vibrant community where aspiring technologists can collaborate, innovate, and make meaningful contributions to society through technology.

Our Journey

A timeline of our milestones and achievements

2020

Foundation Year

JPCS was established with 30 founding members, driven by a shared passion for computer science and technology innovation.

2021

First Annual Hackathon

Hosted our inaugural CodeFest with over 100 participants, establishing JPCS as a key player in the student tech community.

2022

Industry Partnerships

Formed strategic partnerships with leading tech companies, providing internship and mentorship opportunities for members.

2023

National Recognition

Awarded "Best Student Tech Organization" nationally, recognizing our contributions to tech education and innovation.

2024

Community Expansion

Grew to over 500 active members and launched our mentorship program connecting students with industry professionals.

2025

Innovation Hub Launch

Opened our dedicated innovation lab, providing members with state-of-the-art facilities for projects and collaboration.
